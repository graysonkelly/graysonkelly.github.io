Spicy Pickled Eggs - Under Construction

[Description]

  • ⏲️ Prep time: 30 min

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Habenero Peppers
  • Whole Black Peppercorns
  • Fresh Dill
  • Salt
  • Ice

Cookware

  • Half Gallon Jars
  • A pot large enough to submerge your jars
  • A medium cooking pot
  • A large bowl
  • A knife

Directions

  1. Fill the large pot with enough water to completely submerge your glass jars. Bring to boil over high heat. Once boiling, submerge your washed jars and lids to disinfect.
  2. Fill the medium pot with enough water to submerge your eggs. Bring to boil over high heat. In the meanwhile, fill your large bowl with ice and water. Once the water is boiling, submerge eggs and cook to desired doneness. We recommend around 8 minutes for medium-boiled eggs
